How To Play

Deciding Order Of Play:

  • Everyone must roll one die.
  • The person who rolls the highest goes first.
  • Lowest goes last.
  • The rest are in order (high -> low).
  • If there are duplicates, each rolls again and repeat.
    •

Point Rules:

  • 1s: 100pts (x1), 200pts (x2), 1000pts (x3)
    • 3 or more multiply by 2 (2000, 4000, etc).
      •
  • 2s ==> 2 or less (0pts), 200pts (x3)
    • 3 or more multiply by 2 (400, 800, etc).
      •
  • 3s ==> 2 or less (0pts), 300pts (x3)
    • 3 or more multiply by 2 (600, 1200, etc).
      •
  • 4s ==> 2 or less (0pts), 400pts (x3)
    • 3 or more multiply by 2 (800, 1600, etc).
      •
  • 5s ==> 50pts (x1), 100pts (x2), 500pts (x3)
    • 3 or more multiply by 2 (1000, 2000, etc).
      •
  • 6s ==> 2 or less (0pts), 600pts (x3)
    • 3 or more multiply by 2 (1200, 2400, etc).
      •
  • Straight: 1200pts (i.e. 1 2 3 4 5 6)
  • 3 Pairs: 600pts (i.e. 1 1 3 3 6 6)
    •

Playing The Game:

  • FIRST ROUND -> Must score at least 1000pts to keep score!
  • First player will roll all 6 dice.
  • Check for single 1s and 5s, three or more-of-a-kinds, or straights.
  • Set aside scoring dice and roll again.
  • If all dice have rolled scoring points, roll all again (do not end turn).
  • Ending your turn:
    • First round -> MUST have at least 1000pts. If not 0 points for that round.
    • After 1000+ points -> Player can end turn whenever they attain points for that roll.
    • Any points available are added up and applied to current score.
      •
  • All players repeat above steps in determined order.
    •

Winning The Game:

  • First player to score 10,000pts wins.
  • Each other player gets one extra attempt to beat that player's score if possible
